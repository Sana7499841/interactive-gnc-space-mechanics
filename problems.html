Featured Problem

Designing a Hohmann Transfer: Beyond the Equation

Given two circular orbits, determine the transfer trajectory and Δv required. This problem goes beyond the textbook solution by examining assumptions, physical interpretation, and implementation considerations.

  • What assumptions make Hohmann optimal?
  • When does it fail in real missions?
  • How does Δv relate to energy, not just velocity change?
