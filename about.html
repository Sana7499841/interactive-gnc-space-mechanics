Who Am I?

I’m Sana, an aerospace engineer with a strong interest in Guidance, Navigation & Control (GNC) and space mechanics. My work combines physics-based modelling, machine learning, and system-level thinking to understand and predict complex dynamical systems .

This project began as a collection of my own study notes and solved problems — a way to deeply understand the subject by building from first principles. Over time, it evolved into a structured platform to help others develop intuition through clear explanations, practical tools, and real engineering context.

Rather than focusing only on theory, I aim to connect equations to real-world systems — how things actually behave, fail, and get designed in practice.

What You'll Find Here

  • Worked examples explained step by step
  • Simple tools to build intuition and verify calculations
  • Short theory notes that connect equations to real physical meaning

Future Vision

With time, I hope to grow this into a small Interactive GNC & Space Mechanics hub— featuring visualizations, mission-style practice problems, and simple tools that help learners strengthen their understanding of spacecraft dynamics.
