Who Am I?

I’m Sana, an engineer who enjoys exploring Guidance, Navigation & Control and space mechanics. I’ve always believed that complex ideas become meaningful when they are explained simply and shared openly.

This project began as a collection of my own study notes and solved problems—something I built to understand the subject better myself. Over time, it grew into a small initiative to help other learners who might appreciate clear steps, intuitive explanations, and a friendly entry point into GNC.

My goal isn’t to present myself as an expert, but simply as someone who loves learning and hopes these resources make someone else’s journey a little easier.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Worked examples explained step by step
  • Small tools to build intuition and verify calculations
  • Short theory notes that connect equations to real physical meaning

Future Vision

With time, I’d like to grow this into a small interactive GNC & Space Mechanics hub—featuring visualisations, mission-style practice problems, and simple tools that help learners strengthen their understanding.
